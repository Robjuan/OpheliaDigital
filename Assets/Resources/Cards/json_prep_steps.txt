1) go to notion and export the appropriate "Cards" page as CSV
2) go to any csv to json converter (https://www.convertcsv.com/csv-to-json.htm)
3) IMPORTANT: use the setting "Add Top Level Property Name" and enter "cards" 
 - this is crucial and case sensitive etc
4) download and save in Assets/Resources/Cards/<factionname>
5) rename to cards.json
